ICTAE 2024

The ICTAE 2024 Conference is a multidisciplinary forum that brings together academics, industry leaders, researchers, technocrats, and experts from the fields of Computer Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical & Civil Engineering to explore and discuss technological advances and innovations. The conference also provides an opportunity for young researchers to present their works to an international audience, receive feedback from peers around the world, learn from the expertise of leaders in the field, and establish new research collaborations.

The ICTAE 2024 envisions as a facilitator of information and research sharing, analytics, and collaboration by employing innovative methodologies and fostering connections on a global scale. The aim of the mission is to provide, organise, implement, and distribute materials and research that improve the growth and influence of education about the conference theme.

The conference's objective is to advance research and development in various sectors, and to facilitate the sharing of scientific knowledge between foreign and Indian researchers, developers, engineers, students, and practitioners. In other words, the ICTAE 2024 Conference is a major international conference that offers a forum for academics and professionals from a wide range of disciplines to share their works on the latest advances in technology. The conference also aims to promote collaboration and networking between researchers and practitioners from around the world.

CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is a premier private university located in Gujarat, India. It is awarded ‘Grade A+’ by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bangalore. CHARUSAT has been accorded status of Center of Excellence (CoE) by the Government of Gujarat.

CHARUSAT offers various programs, viz., UG, PG, Doctoral, Post-Doctoral, Diploma, Value-added and Executive Development Programs under the tutelage of 9 Institutes, 7 Faculties, and various Centers/Cells. The programs are offered in the allied disciplines of Technology & Engineering, Pharmacy, Computer Applications, Management Studies, Sciences, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Humanities and other Paramedical Sciences.

To know more about CHARUSAT      

Chandubhai S Patel Institute of Technology(CSPIT), is the first institute established in year 2000 at Education Campus, Changa (now CHARUSAT). The institute is managed through a think tank of academicians, scientists, engineers, and professionals from all parts of the world. Started with 240 seats with four Bachelor Degree Engineering Programs in the year 2000, the institute now has a total intake of 600 seats in 7 programs of Bachelor Degree and 72 seats of Post Graduate Degree.

Devang Patel Institute of Advance Technology and Research (DEPSTAR) is a constituent of CHARUSAT. The institute is managed through a think tank of technocrats, administrators, scientists and engineers, educationalists, businessmen, stakeholders and other well-wishers from all parts of the world. DEPSTAR has total intake of 300 Seats with Computer Engineering (120 seats), Computer Science & Engineering (120 seats) and Information Technology (60 seats). The average experience of faculties is more than 6 years in Teaching, Industry and Research Domain.

Call For Papers

  • Prospective authors from India and abroad are invited to submit manuscripts reporting original unpublished research and recent developments in the topics related to the conference.
  • Length of Paper: Minimum 10 pages. Papers need to be submitted in the .docx format.
  • All papers will be double blind reviewed.
  • The ICTAE 2024 organizers regard plagiarism as a serious professional misconduct. All submissions will be screened for plagiarism and when identified, the submissions will be rejected.
  • A submission SHOULD NOT be intended to get reviews from the reviewers for the sole purpose of improving on the quality of the paper. A submission implies that the author intends to ultimately register the paper upon a favorable response from the conference organizers. ICTAE 2024 doesn’t encourage withdrawals after the paper is accepted.
  • Authors should submit their papers online. We use CMT system for submission of papers and review process. Unregistered authors should first create an account on the CMT to log in. For clarifications, contact Dr. Chirag Patel, (+91-7016945563).
  • Please upload your full length paper through the CMT system. Please include the name of the authors, affiliation, and contact number at the top of the full length paper.


Note:
  • The Conference will check plagiarism for all the articles before prior publication. If the plagiarism content is observed by the Technical Committee, peer reviewers or by editors at any stage of the publication process at the time of editing or proofreading will be informed to author to rewrite the manuscript.
  • We also check for plagiarism and research fabrication (using Turnitin license software) and reserve the right to use plagiarism detecting software to screen submitted papers at all times; falsification (manipulation of existing research data, tables, or images). In accordance with the code of conduct we will report any cases of suspected plagiarism or duplicate publishing.
  • In any case if the submitted original manuscript is more than 10% plagiarized then the article will be rejected and the same will be notified to the author.
